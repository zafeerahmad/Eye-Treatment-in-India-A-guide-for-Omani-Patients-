Eye Treatments Available in India

LASIK Surgery: LASIK surgery is a procedure used to correct vision problems, such as nearsightedness, farsightedness, and astigmatism. India has several experienced LASIK surgeons who use advanced technology to perform the procedure.
Glaucoma Treatment: Glaucoma is a group of eye conditions that can lead to blindness if left untreated. India has several experienced glaucoma specialists who use the latest technology to diagnose and treat the condition.
Corneal Transplantation: Corneal transplantation is a surgical procedure that involves replacing the damaged or diseased cornea with a healthy one. India has several renowned corneal transplant surgeons who use the latest technology to perform the procedure.
Retina Treatment: Retina treatment involves treating conditions that affect the retina, such as macular degeneration, diabetic retinopathy, and retinal detachment. India has several experienced retina specialists who use advanced technology to diagnose and treat these conditions.
